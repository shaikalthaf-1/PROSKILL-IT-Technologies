About PROSKILL IT Technologies

Empowering Careers Since 2023

Established in 2023 and led by Founder & CEO Shaik Althaf, PROSKILL IT Technologies has quickly risen to become India's top training and career transformation hub.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic education and industry standards. With campuses in major tech centers like Hyderabad (Ameerpet) and Bangalore, we offer learners state-of-the-art infrastructure, hands-on lab environments, and direct exposure to current real-world challenges.

"Our goal is not just to teach code, but to engineer successful careers. We ensure every candidate receives individual support and high-quality coaching to thrive in the modern IT landscape."
